Wallets

Pennykoin[PK] Wallets are your apps enabling you to use the power of blockchain and transact in your every day life.

﻿​

They are just like your banking apps which you use to check your balance, send or receive money, deposit funds to a saving account, or send secure messages to anyone in the PK Network.

﻿​

There are different Wallets available and we will work hard to include any 3rd party wallet which will start to support Pennykoin[PK]. 

﻿​

We will strive to include as many devices as possible and add them once we are happy with the wallet stability and functionality.
